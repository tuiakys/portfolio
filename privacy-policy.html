Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting my website. I'd like to inform you that by using this site, you agree to accept the use of Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity cookies for analysis and improvement of services.

Google Analytics cookies collect information such as visited pages, time spent, and clicks, in an anonymized and aggregated form.

The Google Analytics cookie collects information such as:

  • Visited pages: records which pages of the site were accessed by the user.
  • Time spent: records how long the user stayed on each page.
  • Traffic source: records how the user arrived at the site, whether through organic search, referral from another site, or marketing campaigns.
  • Clicks: records which elements of the site were clicked by the user.
  • Geographic location: records the approximate location of the user based on IP address.
  • Device type: records the type of device used by the user, such as computer, smartphone, or tablet.
  • Operating system: records the operating system used by the user, such as Windows, iOS, or Android.
  • Browser: records the browser used by the user, such as Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.

Additionally, I also use Microsoft Clarity cookies, which collect additional information for site performance analysis, such as:

  • Mouse movements and clicks: are recorded to understand how users interact with the content and improve usability.
  • Scrolling data: records the scroll position to evaluate the relevance and interest in different content on our site.
  • Time spent on each page: is collected to understand the relevance of the content and improve the user experience.

These informations are used for analysis and understanding of user behavior, allowing me to make improvements to the site, optimize the user experience, and target marketing strategies more effectively. It is important to note that all this information is collected anonymously and aggregated, without personally identifying the users.

We also inform you that we store the information provided, such as your email address and message sent through the form, for an indefinite period unless you expressly request the deletion of this information. We emphasize that your information is treated with confidentiality and appropriate protection. We do not share your information with third parties unless necessary to comply with legal obligations.

If you wish to have your information deleted from our records, please contact us, and we will be happy to accommodate your request.
